About Me

Resume

Hello, My name is Demetri Dillard. I was born and raised in Brooklyn, New York City along with seven of my Siblings; 4 Sisters, 3 Brothers, and me being the middle child. I lived in Brooklyn until the age of 14 when I decided to move to Minnesota after visiting the summer of 1996. In Minnesota, I attended Como Senior High School in St. Paul where I graduated in 2001. I then went on to working various jobs in the field of laborer, carpentry, and sales for a few years which led to my current job of 13 years working for Maplewood School District as a Custodial Engineer.

Growing up from humble beginnings has taught me to be very resilient, self-motivated, and responsible. I have always been a very inquisitive person wanting to learn how things work which has caused me to self-study many different skill sets such as Djing, Japanese, Stock Trading, and now computer programming just to mention a few. In the past, I attended ITT Technical College in the study of IT Networking. There, is where I found my passion for Computer Programming. I managed to get a year of schooling under my belt but wasn't satisfied with the curriculum and the teaching practices at ITT, so I decided to focus on work and building one of my passions into a home business; Djing that is. A self-taught DJ, I was now running an at-home business with mediocre success. With the extra income, I managed to pay off all of my debt and wanted to invest in myself further, particularly in the field of Programming.

After months of researching schools and programs, a friend told me about The University of Minnesota Coding Bootcamp. I've looked into these boot camps in the past but always thought they were a waste of time or even a scam. Now this one was different, it was accredited by a very reputable University so I decided to join. Going forward, I would like to pursue a career in Software Development. I feel that I have a lot to bring to this field because I am eager to learn challenging and difficult task.
